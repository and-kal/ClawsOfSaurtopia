So honoured to have PETER JAMES TAYLOR coming to Leipzig again for the Claws Of Saurtopia noise fest! PJT plays in Bletchley’s noise orchestra ACTION BEAT and does these crazy congregations in the tradition of Glenn Branca, Rhys Chatham and maybe Karlheinz Stockhausen, where he invites a ton of guitarists to play under his guidance. Debris of guitar chords and overtones will ensue!
