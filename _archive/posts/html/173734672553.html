One will never go wrong with a bit of French noise. So, accordingly, here’s HORST DU NOCH (sic!), who’ll play claws Of Saurtopia on June 2. One-man blackened noise punk from the city of Rennes. So, if you liked bands like Teledetente 666 or Headwar in previous editions of Claws of Saurtopia: this is for you!
